Hello and welcome to Cat and Mouse

The aim of the game is to protect the Mice. The Mice live in the box below. Cats will aproach the box from the side of the screen to eat the mice. However the Cats are afraid of the mouse pointer.

You must scare the Cats away with your pointer to protect the Mice

Click to start

Tip: You can 'chase' the Cats away with your pointer and 'terrify' them giving you +20 points!
